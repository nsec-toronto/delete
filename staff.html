Meet the NSEC Doctors


We welcome referrals from family phsycians and specialists.
Our professional staff will make every effort to ensure the best result possible for every patient with minimum discomfort and rapid recovery. Every effort is made to accommodate urgent referrals. Services are completely covered by OHIP with no additional fees.

Patient referrals can be faxed to us at: (416) 293-9402

Our endoscopy clinic carries a fleet of up-to-date Olympus Pediatric and Adult colonoscopes and gastroscopes with capabilities for Image Capture for photodocumentation. Medivator Sterilization System guarantees the highest level of sterilization.
